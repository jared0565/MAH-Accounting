Sectors We Serve

Specialized financial strategy and compliance for dynamic industries across the UK.

Tech & Startups

We understand the rapid growth trajectory of tech startups. From securing SEIS/EIS advance assurance to claiming R&D tax credits, we provide the robust financial foundation necessary for scaling and attracting investment.

Navigating the complex intersection of finance and technology requires specialized knowledge. We support FinTech firms with stringent financial reporting, tax structuring, and audit readiness tailored to this fast-paced environment.

Compliance is non-negotiable. We provide rigorous Statutory Audits and extensive CASS Client Money audits for financial services firms, ensuring complete adherence to Financial Conduct Authority regulations.
